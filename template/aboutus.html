About Us

CandyJP is a company that sells Japanese candies online and ships the product all over the world. The types of products range from modern DIY gummy kits to traditional wagashi eaten in Japanese tea ceremonies. Potential customers could be people who enjoy food or have a sweet tooth. By purchasing from CandyJP, candy lovers get an opportunity to try something that is different than what they normally would get in their countries.
