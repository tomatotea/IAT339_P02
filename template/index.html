Kyoto Wagashi Workshop

Kyoto is famous for its beautiful and delicate wagashi - Japanese traditional handmade confectioneries. Enjoy a delightful evening where you can create your own colourful wagashi served with a bowl of freshly whisked matcha. Come join us at the Vancouver Convention Centre!

Japanese Tea Ceremony

Want to learn more about Japan? Matcha has been an important part of Japanese art and culture since the 1100s. Traditionally enjoyed as a whisked beverage made during the Japanese tea ceremony, matcha is more commonly used nowadays to make sweet lattes and desserts. We'll be holding a ceremony at the next Anime Revolution. Book early!
